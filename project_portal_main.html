✔️ Gantt Planning Criteria Addressed

  • Feasibility Validated: Each initiative (Hackathon, Donate for Health) is broken down with clear timeline, ownership, dependencies, and milestone targets to ensure execution is realistic.
  • Resource Allocation: RACI roles and volunteer/sponsor logistics are layered in the plan. External dependencies (e.g. influencers, developers, transport) are accounted for with time buffers.

    • Time – Clearly defined sprints and deadlines
    • Resources – People, budget, material, partners mapped in context
    • Quality – Milestones (e.g. MVP, social reach, nutrition diversity) mark success standards
